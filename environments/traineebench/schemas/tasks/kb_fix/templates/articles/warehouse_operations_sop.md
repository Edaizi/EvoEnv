# Warehouse Operations SOP

This standard operating procedure (SOP) describes the workflows that maintain safety, accuracy, and throughput in warehouse operations.

## Receiving

- Appointment scheduling and dock assignment
- Inspection: damage, count, documentation
- Putaway: location assignment based on velocity and compatibility

## Inventory Control

- Cycle counts by ABC classification
- Reconciliation process for variances
- Lot and serial tracking; FEFO/FIFO policies

## Picking & Packing

- Wave vs. batch picking by order profile
- Error-proofing: scan validation, weigh verification, photo capture
- Packaging guidelines for fragile and regulated goods

## Shipping

- Carrier selection rules; label generation and manifests
- Cutoff times, exceptions, and weekend policies
- Handover and proof-of-pickup capture

## Safety & Compliance

- OSHA guidelines training; PPE requirements
- Equipment inspections and incident reporting
- Hazardous materials handling procedures

## Metrics & Continuous Improvement

- Lines picked per labor hour; dock-to-stock time; order accuracy
- Root-cause analysis of defects; kaizen events
- Layout optimization and slotting reviews each quarter

## Systems

- WMS responsibilities and access roles
- Integration with ERP, TMS, and returns portal
- Backup procedures during outages

## Conclusion

Operational excellence in the warehouse compounds into better customer experience and lower costs.
